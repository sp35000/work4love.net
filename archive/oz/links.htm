    • Youth Hostel Australia - Sydney Central - This is the best youth hostel I have already seen and I can recommend it. It is a building with 9 floors, electronic access to the bedrooms, sauna, swimming pool, an excellent restaurant with breakfast, lunch and dinner, a big living room, Internet access and Scubar, the hostel's pub. It is not expensive, nice people working there and it is located near the Central Station, so it is close to many places. You can book by e-mail: sydcentral@yhansw.org.au. I'm not earning comissions to indicate them, I just enjoyed the place.

  • These services are really interesting for people travelling, studying and working overseas to communicate with their relatives:
    • Faxaway - Using your e-mail account, you can send a fax to any fax machine in the world for a low price.
    • Net2Phone - If you have a computer with multimedia resources, this service permits you to call any phone number in the world for low rates and the person called don't have to own a computer.
    • Bigpond - This is the Internet Service Provider I used in Australia.
